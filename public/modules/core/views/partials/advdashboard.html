Advertisers & Campaigns

An Advertiser is a top-level entity, and can contain many Campaigns. For example, "Coca-Cola" would be an advertiser, "Summer 2016" would be a campaign. All targeting & creative settings are stored in Campaigns. Use the menu items on the left to create, view & edit your Advertiser & Campaign settings.
