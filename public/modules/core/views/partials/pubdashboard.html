Publishers & Sites

A Publisher is a top-level entity, and can contain many Sites. For example, "Conde Nast" would be a Publisher, "GQ" would be a Site. Most of the settings that actually do anything (bid floors, blacklists, placements, etc.) are stored in Sites, and their child Pages. Use the menu items on the left to create, view & edit your Publisher & Site settings.
