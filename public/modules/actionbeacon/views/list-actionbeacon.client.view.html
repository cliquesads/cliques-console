Action Beacons

Action beacons (A.K.A "tracking pixels" or "conversion pixels") are invisible 1x1 pixels that can be installed on any page to let you track downstream actions users take on your website.

For example, if you want to track how many people who clicked on your Cliques ads later added items to their shopping cart on your site, you would create an action beacon called Add to Cart and place it in the code controlling your add to cart button. For more information, please see our support center.
