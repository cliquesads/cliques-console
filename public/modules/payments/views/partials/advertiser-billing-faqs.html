Billing FAQs

What is your billing cycle?

Monthly. Cliques will generate an invoice on the 1st of each month reflecting prior month's billable impressions & applicable fees.


When are payments due?

15 days after the invoice date. The total balance reflected in each invoice is due no later than 15 days following the invoice date.

If you are using a credit card, your card will be billed automatically, so you don't need to worry about submitting payments manually.

Note: We require Net 15 terms in order to ensure that we can pay Cliques publishers in full no later than 30 days after publisher statements are generated. Cliques doesn't work without happy publishers, and publishers aren't happy if they're getting paid late!


What happens if I'm late on a payment?

As per section 3.2 of the Advertiser Terms and Conditions, a late fee shall accrue at the rate of the lesser 1.5% per month or the highest legal rate permitted by law. Absolutely not. Cliques uses to validate, store & process all credit card information.
